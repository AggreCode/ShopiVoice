## Usage

1. Generate TTS Dataset:
   ./run_training_pipeline.sh 5000

2. Or manual:
   python3 test_xtts_indian.py --dataset-size 5000 --output-dir output

3. Check output:
   ls output/audio/train/wavs/
   cat output/audio/train/metadata.csv
